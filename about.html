Origin

"Root Cause" was founded by Michigan State University student Derek Viera. Root Cause is a streetwear clothing brand that creates pieces that embody
current and powerful trends and designs, we are driven to create pieces that portray our unique and aesthetic designs.

Founded in October of 2022, Root Cause provides high-quality and affordable clothing for people of all ages around the world.
We at Root Cause believe in creating collections that demonstrate our intention and longevity with timeless pieces.
